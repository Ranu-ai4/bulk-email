Stratify — What's New

Self-signup, onboarding, Chat Bot, Management Reports, and more.

Here's what's new in Stratify this release — self-signup, a getting started guide, Chat Bot, management reports, and a lot of stability improvements.

🔐

New

Self-Signup & Domain Whitelisting

  • Users with whitelisted email domains can now sign up directly from the login page
  • Admins manage allowed domains in Settings
  • All users can change their own password via Settings
🚀

New

Getting Started Guide

  • New onboarding page with a step-by-step workflow guide and decision tree
  • Key definitions accordion for quick reference
  • Dismissable banner on the Dashboard to guide new users
💬

New

Chat Bot

Ask questions about your organisation's processes and assessments. Powered by access-controlled RAG over your data in MongoDB.

📊

New

Management Reports & Analytics

  • 6-tab reports dashboard — scorecard, comparison, trends, insights, ROI, and export
  • Effort-benefit scatter chart and ROI table in the Management Report tab
  • FTE and process volume inputs now feed into LLM analysis calculations
🎯

Improved

Smarter Assessments

  • Per-criteria AI scoring with reasoning — hover any criteria to see why it scored the way it did
  • Resume interrupted interviews — progress is saved and restored automatically
  • Professional multi-step loading animation during AI analysis
🛡️

Security

Stability & Security

  • Auto-save for process documentation — drafts saved every 10 seconds, recovered on reload
  • Delete confirmations on all destructive actions across the app
  • RBAC enforcement — admin-only mutations, scoped assessment visibility
  • Activity logging for full audit trail of user actions
